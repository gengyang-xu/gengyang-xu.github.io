About me
======
I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of [Prof. Shuai Wang](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~shuaiw/). Prior to this, I earned my BSc in Computer Science with first-class honors from Hong Kong Baptist University, where I was advised by [Prof. DAI, Henry Hong Ning](https://www.comp.hkbu.edu.hk/v1/?page=profile&id=henrydai).

My research focuses on **Programming Language, Software Engineering, Security**.

Contact: gxuah [at] cse [dot] ust [dot] hk
